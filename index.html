About This Website

Classical Genre Definition

"a style of music composed, esp at Vienna, during the late 18th and early 19th centuries. This period is marked by the establishment, esp by Haydn and Mozart, of sonata form "

"any style of music based on long-established principles of composition and polyphony and marked by stability of form, intellectualism, and restraint."

Classical music can cover many different types of music that can fall under the categorie "Classical Genre" such as :-

  • Avant Garde
  • Chamber Music
  • Chant
  • Opera
  • Renaissance
  • Romantic
  • Classical/Crossover

This site hopes to be developed with such artists that have started out as classical and may still be in this genre, but some of the artists have crossed over into other genre. Future developement of this stite hopes to include and not exclude any genre.
